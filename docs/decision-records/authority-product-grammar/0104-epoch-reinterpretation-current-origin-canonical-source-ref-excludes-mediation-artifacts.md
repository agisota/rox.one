# Decision 0104: Epoch Reinterpretation Current Origin Canonical Source Ref Excludes Mediation Artifacts

- Status: accepted
- Date: 2026-04-25

## Canonical
```text
current_origin canonical_source_ref denotes:
  immediate provenance anchor
  of the frontier origin

the anchor is:
  source-side
  lineage-facing
  provenance-bearing

canonical_source_ref does not denote:
  conflict_resolution artifact
  adoption artifact
  provenance-promotion artifact
  other mediation-only transition record
```

## Why
- Keeps immediate provenance interpretation anchored to source lineage while leaving adjudication and transition mechanics on the artifact side of the model.
