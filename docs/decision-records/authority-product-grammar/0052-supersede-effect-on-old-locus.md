# Decision 0052: Supersede Effect On Old Locus

- Status: accepted
- Date: 2026-04-25

## Canonical
```text
when locus A is superseded by locus B:
  locus A loses
  canonical foreground role

  locus A becomes
  superseded

  locus A remains
  inspectable in history

  locus A does not stay
  active-primary
```

## Why
- Makes replacement explicit without erasing the old frame from history.
